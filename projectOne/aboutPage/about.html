Who Am I?

My name is Kwok Ren. I am Chinese American and I was born and raised in NYC through my entire life. I went to Baruch College, where I majored in Accounting. Recently, I wanted to learn some programming languages and I decided to pursue higher education for software engineering. I am currently a student in General Assembly, and I am pursuing a career in Web Development.
My mission as a web developer is to create online platforms that will impact people’s lives in a meaningful way. I hope to use my abilities to not only create beautiful websites that are easy to navigate through, but to also inspire people to do more.
